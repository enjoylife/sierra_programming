futures contracts
    def- agreement to buy or sell an asset at a cetrain time in the future for a certain price.

    exchanges- Chicago Board of Trade, Chicago Mercantile Exchange,
            London International Financial Futures and Options Exchange, and Eurex

buying investor-"long futures"
Selling person-"short futures position"

prices are determined as usual- "by supply and demand"

lots of sellers- Lowers price
more people that want to buy- Raises price
